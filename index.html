About Reel Developer

Welcome to Reel Developer, where I'm creating the next generation of interactive, entertaining, and engaging games!

My name is Daphne Link, and I'm a seasoned software engineer with a passion for games and a desire to build my own business. At Reel Developer, I'm not just developing games – I'm building a community of like-minded developers who want to learn, grow, and create together.

My monthly Q&A and game development trainings are just the beginning. By joining my community, you'll have the chance to see behind the scenes of game development and get a firsthand look at how a studio is built from the ground up. Plus, you'll be able to connect with other developers and share your own unique perspective on the world of gaming.

I believe in the power of diversity and representation, and I think it should be at the forefront of the games we play. My mission and the Reel Developer mission is to offer players the opportunity to see themselves and their experiences reflected in the narratives and characters of interactive, entertaining and engaging games. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting out, I want you to be a part of the journey.

So don't wait – join the Reel Developer community today and start making your mark on the world of gaming!

Our Community

The Reel Developer community is not only about me, but it is about you and the collective. Together we believe a strong community is essential to the success of any game studio. That's why Discord was chosen as the platform for the community. Not only is it a familiar tool for many gamers and developers, but it's also easy to use, versatile and provides real-time communication. You'll be able to connect with like-minded developers, share your experiences, and learn from others in a seamless and engaging way.

Don't miss out on the opportunity to be a part of the community, join today and start your journey with us!
