About Reel Developer

Welcome to Reel Developer!

The idea for Reel Developer started in 2022 from a love of movies and games, where the name was inspired by using movie trailers as inspiration to create games. In 2026, the studio officially launched.

The founder is a software engineer and lifelong game creator, now building her own studio.

We're here to make original games for a diverse audience that are fun to play and fun to build. While building the studio, our focus is on sharing the journey and building tools that help others establish their online presence and ship their first game.

Indie games, indie life.
